\begin{definition}[Reduced Fraction]\index{Reduced Fraction}\label{eq:rf}
A fraction of the form $\frac{m}{n}$, where $n \ne 0$, is said to be in it's reduced form if the $\gcd(m,n)=1$
\end{definition}
